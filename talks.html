Invited Speakers

Fei Huang

Dr. Fei Huang is a Principal Engineer / Senior Director at Language Technology Lab, Alibaba Group. He holds a Ph.D. from Carnegie Mellon University, USA. He is focusing on multi-modal MT (IM, speech, image and video translation), unsupervised MT, etc. Before joining Alibaba, he led the MT Research at Facebook Applied Machine Learning Department. He was a senior researcher at IBM Watson. he has conducted research in multilingual NLP, machine translation, information extraction and speech recognition. He published numerous papers at AAAI, ACL, EMNLP, NAACL, etc., served as area chairs at ACL and NLPCC, senior program committee member and reviewers of many journals and conferences.
